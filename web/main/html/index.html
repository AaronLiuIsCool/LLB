About us

Little Leaf baby is a retail store located at Windermere Currents, Edmonton. We're selling baby products including feeding, bath, health and safety, shoes and clothing and toys and related accessories for baby.

Like all the other mom, I only choose the best quality and cute fashion product for my baby. For reaching this, I have to run to different stores and order worldwide to get the product that I want. Then I came up this idea. Why wouldn't I bring all the shinning starts together and share to all other moms.

I am really hoping you enjoy shopping in Little Leaf Baby. You would never be disappointed by our products and services.
